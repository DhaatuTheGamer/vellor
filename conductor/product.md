# Vellor Product Guide

## Initial Concept
Vellor is a free, open-source Progressive Web App built for private teachers and tutors. It provides a single, beautifully designed interface to manage students, track lessons and payments, generate invoices, and stay motivated through built-in gamification — all while keeping your data 100% private on your own device.

## Target Audience
The primary target users are **Independent Tutors and Small Tutoring Businesses**. These are educators who need simple, local-first management without the complexity of large SaaS platforms, but with enough power to handle their growing operations.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Absolute Privacy:** 100% offline, local-first data control using encrypted `localStorage`.
- **Frictionless UX:** Zero-config, fast, mobile-ready PWA experience designed for immediate use.
- **Financial Clarity:** Powerful dashboards and one-click PDF invoicing to keep track of earnings seamlessly.

## Feature Focus & Roadmap Priorities
For the immediate future, Vellor will focus on:
- **Gamification & Engagement:** Enhancing the tutor motivation loop with more achievements and milestones to prevent burnout.
- **Client Communication:** Building better WhatsApp integrations and read-only client portals for parents to check progress and attendance.
